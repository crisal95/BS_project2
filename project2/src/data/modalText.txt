Welcome to Do you want to play a game? Trivia!

Rules:

- You have to choose a category.
- The game consist on 10 questions about the same category.
- You are going to have just 30 secons to answer.
- With every right answer, you are going to win a bigger reward.
- If your answer is correct, you will be able to choose between giving up or keep playing.
- If you answer correctly and you give up, you keep your rewards.
- If your answer is incorrect, you lose your rewards.
- If your time is up, you lose the game and your rewards. 
- If you answer correctly all the 10 questions, you are going to win 5 millon dollars.

May the odds be ever in your favor!!
